## Sequencing logic

1. **Now → 90 days:** anchor on **Accel Atoms** (pre-seed friendly, dev/AI thesis). In parallel, file the two **low-risk, equity-free** options — **NSRCEL** and **Google for Startups India** — since they cost almost nothing and add credibility + credits.
2. **After first traction signal (weekly-active India devs trending up):** apply to **Surge** and consider **Antler** (Bangalore residency = no relocation).
3. **YC:** apply opportunistically if a batch lines up; treat any offer as a decision to make *then*, never as the plan.

## What "ready" means for the #1 (Accel Atoms)
- Live, public product anyone can use in <60s (Plan 5 deployed).
- 20–50 real developers in India having used VOUCH at least once; a handful repeat users or design partners.
- One-sentence wedge + one-sentence "why now."
- 60-second demo video + a 5–8 slide narrative (velocity + wedge, not market-size theatre).
- Founder story: why a Bangalore CS student is the right person to build OSS trust tooling.

## Open questions (decide before applying)
- Solo founder, or recruit a co-founder? (Most India accelerators favour ≥2; have an answer.)
- Full-time vs. study commitment framing (Antler especially will probe this).
- Entity: register now (DPIIT/Startup India recognition is free and unlocks several programs) or wait?
